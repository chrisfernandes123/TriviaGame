Trivia

You will be asked a series of questions. Select the correct answer from the answer options available.
Each question will be timed. You will have 30 seconds to submit your answer.
Upon selecting your answer, you will be immediately notified if you are correct or incorrect.
The next question will automatically appear after 5 seconds.
After all the questions have been answered, you will be shown your results.
Please click the Start button to start the trivia game. Good Luck!
